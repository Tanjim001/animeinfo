The main source of energy is called ki energy. DB charcter use various technices by channelig it.Like goku use kamahamaha as his special attack . Vegata use big bang attack . There are many kind of tranformation differ by species.Sayajin race use super sayajin transformation(ss1,ss2,ss3,ss4) and sayjin god transformation(red and blue).Those transformation boost their power level to extream.Their are many other technices like kaioken,ultra instinct, hakai or destroyer etc.

This anime series was good till dragon ball gt(1996). But after that this anime series is ruined according to me. It doesn't follow good story line and lost logic,just jump in battle.Overall I pointout some cons and perks and listed below.

  1. Good story till DB GT

  3. Power level described well with transformation.
  4. Rivalary system
  5. Children friendly cause it just represent matrial arts and dont have any 18+ scenes or lewd dialogues.
  6. Fascinating background music
